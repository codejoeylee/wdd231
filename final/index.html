What is an STI?

Sexually Transmitted Infections (STIs) are infections that spread through sexual contact. They can be caused by bacteria, viruses, or parasites and may affect anyone, regardless of age or background. Some common STIs include chlamydia, gonorrhea, syphilis, and HIV. Early detection and prevention—through safe practices, regular testing, and education—are key to maintaining sexual health.

Why STI Awareness Matters

Understanding STIs is crucial for maintaining sexual health. Many infections can be prevented, treated, or managed with the right knowledge. Let's break the stigma and empower ourselves with information.
