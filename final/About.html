About STI Aware

STI Aware is your go-to platform for accurate, stigma-free information on sexually transmitted infections (STIs), prevention, testing, and treatment. Our mission is to empower individuals with knowledge, promote safe health practices, and foster open conversations that drive positive change.

Why Sexual Health Education Matters

Understanding STIs, their transmission, and prevention methods helps individuals make informed health choices. Education is key to reducing risks and improving overall well-being.

Destigmatizing Sexual Health

STIs are common, yet misinformation fuels fear and shame. The reality? Sexual health is just health. No one should feel embarrassed about seeking care or discussing prevention. STI Aware works to normalize these conversations and empower people to take control of their health.

How You Can Stay Proactive

  • Routine Testing: Regular STI checkups ensure early detection and proper treatment.
  • Safe Practices: Use condoms, dental dams, and preventive medications to lower risk.
  • Open Conversations: Honest discussions with partners build trust and awareness.
  • Education & Advocacy: Sharing knowledge reduces stigma and misinformation.

Join the Movement

STI Aware is more than a resource—it’s a community pushing for open discussions, better education, and access to sexual health care. Whether you're here to learn, find resources, or support the cause, your voice matters.
